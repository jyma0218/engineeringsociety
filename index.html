Strandbeest Project

Jansen's linkage is a planar leg mechanism designed by the kinetic sculptuor Theo Jansen to generate a walking motion. His kinetic model get their energy from the wind and simulate organic walking motion from a simple rotary input. They evolved over generations, broadening its applications.
